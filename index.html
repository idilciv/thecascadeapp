Choose your path

How will you resist?

Three modes to fit your day. Zen for gentle focus. Hardcore for serious commitment. Vault for the strictest protection.

Zen Mode

Gentle blocking.

Pick the apps you want to block and the session length, then start. If you need to stop, you can stop the session and use the apps you need.

Zen Mode

Set a routine.

Schedule recurring blocks for any day and time. Cascade starts your session automatically.

Hardcore Mode

Customizable blocking.

You block the apps you choose, but you also give yourself a few unlock passes. For example, you can let a blocked app open 3 times for 10 minutes. When they're gone, the app stays blocked until the session ends.

Hardcore Mode

Your rules. Your limits.

Before you start, you set the blocking duration, how many times you can temporarily unlock apps, and how long each unlock window lasts.

Hardcore Mode

Routine on autopilot.

Set recurring Hardcore sessions for the days and times you want. Cascade locks you in automatically when the schedule begins.

Vault Mode

Strict blocking.

You block the apps you choose and you can't stop the session early. The only way in is a limited number of weekly unlock passes that you set yourself beforehand.

Vault Mode

No way out.

Vault sessions cannot be stopped early. The session ends automatically when the time is up. This is the strongest commitment you can make to yourself.

Vault Mode

Lock it in on schedule.

Set recurring Vault sessions for the days and times you choose. Once the schedule kicks in, there's no early exit until the session ends.
