Questions

Everything you might be wondering.

Can I really not unlock Vault mode early?

That's the whole point. In Vault mode, the apps you pick stay sealed for the full session. No snooze, no "just five more minutes." If you'd rather have an easier way out, Zen only nudges you and Hardcore gives you a limited number of passes.

Is my data private?

Yes. Blocking runs on-device with Apple's Screen Time framework, and your focus data stays yours. Cascade is built so your blocked-app choices never leave your phone.

How does the free trial work?

The Yearly plan comes with a 7-day free trial. You won't be charged if you cancel any time before it ends, and you can manage it straight from your Apple ID subscriptions.

Does it keep working when the app is closed?

Absolutely. Blocking and scheduled routines run in the background through Screen Time. Widgets, Live Activities and the Dynamic Island keep your streak and countdown visible without opening the app.

What languages is Cascade available in?

22, fully localized, including English, Türkçe, Español, Deutsch, Français, 日本語, 한국어, 简体中文, العربية and many more.

Can I block specific apps or whole categories?

Both. Pick out individual apps, or block whole categories at once. Whatever works for you.
