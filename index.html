Features About

Coming to iOS

A screen time app you'll love using.

Easy to use, designed to help you take back your time. Block what distracts. Keep what matters.

Features

Built to make things easy.

Every tool you need to cut through the noise and get back to what matters.

🧘
Zen Mode

Apps blocked on schedule. Gentle but firm — pause only if you truly need to.

🔒
Hardcore Mode

No way out. No pause. For when you really need to commit.

📵
App Blocking

Schedule when distracting apps are restricted. Set your hours and use your phone without the noise.

⏱
Focus Streaks

Track your sessions and build a daily habit worth keeping.

✦
Credits System

In Hardcore mode, you have a daily credit limit. Once it's gone, apps are fully blocked — no way around it.

📊
Time Saved

See exactly how much screen time you've reclaimed. Every session counts.

How it works

Simple to start.
Hard to quit.

Cascade is built to get out of your way. Easy to set up. Easy to use.

01

Choose your mode

Zen — block apps on a schedule with timed sessions. Hardcore — once you start, it's almost impossible to unblock.

02

Pick your blocklist

Choose which distracting apps get restricted during your session.

03

Start your session

Begin and let Cascade handle the rest. Watch your streak grow.
